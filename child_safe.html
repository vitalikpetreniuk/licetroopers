All-Natural Head Lice Treatment
Safe for Children

Your child's safety is your first priority. However, so is getting rid of head lice. This is why Lice Troopers is committed to a non-toxic, All-Natural Head Lice Treatment Safe for Children of all ages. Our lice removal method is easy and effective. No harsh chemicals, no prescription shampoos, just the time honored products and methods proven safe, and guaranteed to get rid of the head louse (Pediculus humanus capitis) parasites and nits.

Products we use for Treatment and Removal

All-Natural Head Lice Treatment Lotion

Specially designed to moisturize and strengthen hair, our Treatment Lotion makes it easier to comb out lice and nits. Formulated from only the finest natural ingredients, this lotion is safe for kids of all ages and is even gentle on color-treated hair.

All-Natural Hair Thickener

Our All-Natural Hair Thickener is designed to thicken the hair shaft, trapping nits and making it easier to comb them out in a single swoop. Safe and effective, this product speeds along the removal treatment process.

Speciality Lice Comb

Our state-of-the-art comb, custom manufactured in Germany, features a stainless steel body with fine-toothed pins (narrow spaced long teeth) specifically designed to remove the nits and parasites attached to the hair shaft and head. This #1 rated comb for removal of head louse parasites and nits (eggs), is reusable because it can be sanitized, and comes with a lifetime warranty.

All-Natural Head Lice Prevention Spray

Clean, organic and non-toxic, our prevention spray is formulated with liquid garlic from organic garlic cloves. The garlic smell drives the nits and parasites away. But don’t worry, after a few minutes the smell won’t be noticeable on your child.
