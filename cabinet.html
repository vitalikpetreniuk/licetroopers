Membership terms, billing & cancellation policy

All Lice Troopers memberships plans are on auto-renew as per our enrollment terms and conditions. Your credit card will automatically be re-billed monthly. You will be billed the same amount based on your selected plan. Please be advised that your re-billing date can fluctuate based on your sign up date. To notify us that you do not wish to AUTO-RENEW for a subsequent billing cycle, we require notice in writing at least 4 business days prior to your current membership cycle ending. If you wish not to auto-renew, please write NO AUTO-RENEW in the subject line of your email and send your request to Memberships@LiceTroopers.com.

Please note that if we do not receive notification in writing at least 4 business days before your current program cycle ends, you will automatically be billed for an additional cycle and will be obligated to continue service through the subsequent cycle(s). All memberships become active 45 days after signup. Minimum 12 month commitment.
